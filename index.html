ARCADE GAME: ROGUE ONE

The rebel team of Rogue One, led by Jyn Erso and Cassian Andor, with the help of the repurposed Imperial droid K2-SO, have obtained the plans to the Death Star. Help them get the plans past the Imperial forces on Scarif and into the Stars, where the Rebel Alliance waits. Use the up, down, left, and right arrows on your keyboard to navigate your rebel hero. Beware--six collisions with Imperial forces will result in failure for the rebels. To play again, reload the page.

Game by Emily Keator; based off of Udacity Nanodegree project skeleton code for a clone of the classic arcade game "Frogger". ALL STAR WARS CHARACTERS DO NOT BELONG TO ME.
